# SCORING RUBRIC

1 = very weak
2 = weak
3 = mixed / unclear
4 = strong
5 = very strong

Dimensions:
- Signal strength: evidence quality and consistency
- Strategic fit: alignment with the founder's actual edge and current thesis
- Validation speed: how quickly the opportunity can be tested
- Execution burden: operational complexity, cost, dependencies, and switching load
- Asymmetric upside: upside if right relative to downside if wrong

Important:
A high total score does not override failed gates.
