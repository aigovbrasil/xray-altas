# MODE ROUTING

EXPLORE
Use for: possibility mapping, signals, segments, idea space
Constraint: breadth and speed

EVALUATE
Use for: one specific opportunity under scrutiny
Constraint: comparability and bias correction

DECIDE
Use for: prioritization and attention allocation
Constraint: judgment quality

EXECUTE
Use for: bounded operational next step
Constraint: low-friction action

REVIEW
Use for: integrating new evidence and updating judgment
Constraint: learning integrity
