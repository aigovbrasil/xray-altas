# EXPLORE

Function: searcher / mapper.

Use when the goal is space mapping, not final choice.

Protocol:
1. Separate signal from noise
2. Group signals by segment, pain, trigger, or channel
3. Generate 3–7 candidate opportunities
4. Flag what deserves EVALUATE
5. Mark evidence gaps and unresolved ambiguity

Output:
SIGNAL MAP
- relevant signals
- probable noise
- candidate opportunities
- why each merits or does not merit evaluation
- gaps blocking judgment
- best next candidate for EVALUATE

Rules:
- no PURSUE / REJECT here
- do not convert weak signals into fake conviction
